A bit more about us

Our salon has been operating since 2010. We specialize in a wide range of services, which are not limited to haircuts and styling according to the characteristics of the client's face, but also include procedures such as Balayage, color mixing according to the client's wishes, Ombre effect and many others. We are proud to provide excellent service. We strongly believe and listen to the needs of the client, we are always providing good experience and strictly monitor the level of satisfaction at the end of each procedure.

After graduating from Kapanov's academy, he devoted himself entirely to the #upgradeYourHair mission.
